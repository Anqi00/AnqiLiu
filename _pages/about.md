# Welcome

I am **Anqi Liu**, a researcher working at the intersection of **robotics, computer vision, and intelligent agriculture**.

My research focuses on developing perception and decision-making systems for orchard robots, with particular interests in:

* 3D Point Cloud Processing
* Graph Neural Networks
* Precision Pruning
* Orchard Robotics
* 2D–3D Camera Pose Registration
* Vision-Guided Manipulation

# Research Overview

Modern orchard robots require reliable perception, accurate localization, and biologically informed decision-making. My work aims to bridge these components by integrating point cloud analysis, graph learning, and robotic execution into a unified framework.

Current research topics include:

* Skeleton-based pruning point prediction
* Graph neural networks for branch structure understanding
* 2D–3D registration between orchard maps and online camera views
* Autonomous robotic pruning systems
* Multi-modal perception for agricultural robotics

# Selected Projects

### Context-Pruner

A biologically informed graph neural network framework for pruning point prediction on apple tree skeleton graphs.

### 2D–3D Camera Pose Registration

A registration framework that aligns offline reconstructed orchard models with online camera observations for robotic execution.

### Orchard Robot Platform

Development of a perception and manipulation pipeline for autonomous orchard operations.
